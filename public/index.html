About Us


ExpenseSplit provides people with an easy, free, fast and reliable way to split up their group expenses after holidays, vacations, trips or roadtrips. It smartly manages and keeps track of all shared expenses and balance between friends. We aim to achieve our goals and deliver a perfect solution for each and everyone.
